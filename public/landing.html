The pipeline

Four stages, one autonomous agent

From the moment the bill is ready to the moment the patient pays — Q-Dispatch owns the whole claim.

1

Assembly & Pre-Audit

Ingests the HIS export, then the AI audits every line and predicts the likely approval — before anything is sent.

2

Instant Dispatch

Builds an NHCX-ready HL7 FHIR claim with a tracking token and transmits it — no portal logins.

3

Inbox Surveillance

Reads the insurer's reply automatically and extracts the decision, amounts, and deductions.

4

Counter Clearance

Pushes the exact copay to the counter; the patient pays and leaves. The fee bills to the hospital.
